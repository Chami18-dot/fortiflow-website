FortiFlow Analytics

About Us

FortiFlow Analytics, LLC is a startup technology firm based in Abilene, Texas. We provide integrated data engineering and cybersecurity services to small and mid-sized enterprises (SMEs). Our mission is to empower businesses with secure, efficient, and intelligent data systems that drive innovation and resilience in the digital age.

Our Vision

To become a trusted leader in secure data infrastructure, enabling a safer digital future for organizations worldwide.

Services

  • Data Engineering: ETL pipeline design, data warehousing, cloud architecture, and real-time analytics.
  • Cybersecurity: Vulnerability assessments, secure cloud migration, identity & access management, and compliance support (HIPAA, GDPR).
  • Managed Services: Ongoing infrastructure monitoring and support.

Marketing Strategy

We target SMEs in healthcare, fintech, and e-commerce by offering digital outreach via LinkedIn and webinars, partnerships with cloud providers, and a free vulnerability/data audit for new clients.
